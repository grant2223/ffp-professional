Questions, answered

How do I get the professional dashboard?

Just sign up and get an FFP Passport ($20/month or $149/year). The same login opens your professional dashboard — there's no separate professional account, setup fee or commission.

How do I get paid?

You connect your own Stripe account, so clients pay you directly. FFP never holds your money and payouts are automatic.

Do I control my availability?

Fully. You set your services, open and close slots, and toggle which are bookable online — changes go live to members instantly.

How do members find me?

You appear in the FFP discovery app and are recommended by Grant — our AI guide — based on your specialty, location and availability.

Do I keep what I earn?

Yes — there's no commission on the Professionals plan. Clients pay you directly through your connected account and you keep 100% of every booking.
